Information

Description:

This experiment is a listening test, where you are asked to assign categories to a group of given sounds. The aformentioned categories belong to the new taxonomy we are introducing for sound description in Freesound. The proposed taxonomy is explained before the listening part, in which there are 5 top-level categories with 3-10 subcategories to each.

Instructions:

  • You are asked to:
    • provide some basic demographic data (e.g. if you are freesound user or musician),
    • get familiar with the taxonomy for a few minutes,
    • listen to around ~20 given sounds one by one and choose the best category for each,
    • give text feedback to the taxonomy (optionally).
  • Each sound can be assigned to only one category.
  • It is preferable to be located in a quiet environment.
  • To ensure accurate results, please refrain from using the back button during the experiment.
  • We kindly request you to finish and submit the results, otherwise your answers won't be commited.
I consent on participating in this study and have read the experiment terms.
